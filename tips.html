👁 Eye Care Tips

Take care of your eyes and keep your vision healthy.

✨ Click the Button to Learn Eye Care Tips


    🥗 Healthy Foods for Eyes

    🥕 Carrot

    Rich in Vitamin A. Improves eyesight and keeps eyes healthy.

    🥬 Spinach

    Contains Lutein and Zeaxanthin which protect the retina.

    🍊 Orange

    Vitamin C helps reduce eye damage and keeps eyes fresh.

    🐟 Fish

    Omega-3 fatty acids help prevent dry eyes.

    🥚 Eggs

    Rich in nutrients that support healthy vision.

    🥜 Almonds

    Vitamin E protects the eyes from ageing.

    📅 Daily Eye Care Routine

    1. 👀 Wash your eyes with clean water every morning.
    2. 💻 Follow the 20-20-20 rule while using screens.
    3. 📖 Read in good lighting.
    4. 🥤 Drink plenty of water.
    5. 😴 Sleep at least 7–8 hours daily.
    6. 🕶 Wear sunglasses in bright sunlight.
    7. 👨‍⚕ Visit an eye doctor once a year.

    🚫 Things to Avoid

    ❌ Don't Rub Eyes

    Rubbing can damage the cornea and spread germs.

    📱 Don't Use Mobile Too Long

    Long screen time causes eye strain.

    🌙 Don't Read in Darkness

    Always use proper lighting while reading.

    💤 Don't Ignore Sleep

    Lack of sleep makes your eyes tired and dry.
